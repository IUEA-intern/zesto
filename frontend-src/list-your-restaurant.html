FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before joining Zesto as a restaurant partner.
Talk to our team
How much does it cost to list my restaurant on Zesto? −
Listing your restaurant on Zesto is completely free. We charge a commission only on completed orders — starting at 14% for our Growth plan. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges.
How long does the approval process take? +
Most restaurants are reviewed and approved within 24 hours of submitting their application. Our team will reach out to confirm your details and help you set up your menu and dashboard.
When and how do I receive payments? +
Payouts are made every week directly to your mobile money account (MTN, Airtel) or bank account. You can track all your earnings and transaction history in your partner dashboard in real time.
Do I need to handle my own delivery riders? +
No. Zesto manages the entire delivery network. When a customer places an order, our riders handle pickup and delivery. You focus on preparing great food — we handle the rest.
Can I manage multiple restaurant branches? +
Yes! Our Enterprise plan supports multi-branch management from a single dashboard. You can set different menus, hours, and pricing for each location. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise pricing.
What if I need help after going live? +
Our partner support team is available 7 days a week via chat, phone, and WhatsApp. Growth and Enterprise partners get priority support and a dedicated account manager who checks in regularly.
